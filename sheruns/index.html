SheRuns Club - Website Plan

Site Name

SheRuns Club – This name highlights the focus on women runners and fosters a sense of empowerment, community, and identity for female athletes. It's short, memorable, and meaningful.

Optional domain availability: sherunsclub.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a central hub for female runners to connect, join a supportive community, and find information about local running events. It will include:

  • Club mission and how to join
  • Running schedules and meet-up locations
  • Safety tips for women runners
  • Benefits of group running

Scenarios

  • Where can I find upcoming meet-up times for beginner runners?
  • What safety measures does the club promote for running at night?

Color Schema

  • Main Color (Soft Rose Pink): #f78da7 – used for headers, buttons, and accents
  • Secondary Color (Cool Slate Blue): #475569 – used for backgrounds and footer

Typography

  • Headings: 'Poppins', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Roboto', sans-serif
